    Information about the OET Exam

    What is OET?

    The Occupational English Test (OET) is an international English language test that assesses the language communication skills of healthcare professionals who seek to register and practice in an English-speaking environment.

    Who Needs OET?

    OET is designed for healthcare professionals, including doctors, nurses, dentists, pharmacists, physiotherapists, and more, to prove their English proficiency.

    OET Test Format

    • Listening: 45 minutes – Understand spoken healthcare-related English.
    • Reading: 60 minutes – Comprehend medical and professional texts.
    • Writing: 45 minutes – Write a referral, discharge, or other professional letter.
    • Speaking: 20 minutes – Engage in role-play based on real-life healthcare scenarios.

    Why Take OET?

    • Accepted in countries like the UK, USA, Australia, Canada, and more.
    • Recognized by healthcare boards and councils.
    • Real-world relevance for healthcare professionals.

    For more information, visit the official OET website: www.occupationalenglishtest.org
